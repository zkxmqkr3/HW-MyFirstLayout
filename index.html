About me

UX/UI Designer with a background in Industrial Design. Certificate in UX/UI from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Georgia with skills in Research, Visual Prototyping & Wireframing, User Interface Development, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.

I wanted to become a UX/UI designer, while I was working as a freight forwarding analyst, I recognized a bad designed system or product that could cause unnecessary time consuming and lose the work efficiency. It was the moment that I have come to know the influence of the UX/UI design.

I am passionate about user-center design and make a product that enhances user satisfaction by improving usability and pleasure provided in the interaction.

Also, I work closely with team members and challenges the develops practical products and systems through collection analysis and synthesis of data guided by the special requirements. Industrial design background with strong visual communication skills and always staying top of design trends make me a valuable addition to any team.
